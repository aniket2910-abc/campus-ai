Application Process

1. Apply online through student portal during admission
2. Submit supporting documents (marksheets, income certificate, sports certificates)
3. Scholarship committee reviews applications
4. Personal interview (for Sports & Financial categories)
5. Results announced within 15 days of application deadline
6. Scholarship applied directly to fee invoice
Deadline: July 31st, 2026. Late applications not accepted.

Important Notes

  • Scholarship renewal requires maintaining 8.0+ CGPA each semester
  • Attendance must be above 80% for scholarship continuation
  • No disciplinary action during the academic year
  • Financial aid recipients must submit income proof annually
  • Sports scholars must represent the college in tournaments
